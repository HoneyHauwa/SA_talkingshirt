About us

Artist & Designer

Welcome to Talkingshirt, where fashion meets self-expression. As founder Salt Victor, I’m passionate about empowering individuals to make a statement with their clothing. I believe our clothing can be a powerful tool for self-expression, and I’m committed to creating high-quality, thought-provoking pieces that spark conversations and inspire connections. Join me on this journey to redefine fashion and self-expression.”let’s get it.

Vision:To become a leading fashion brand that empowers individuals to express themselves authentically, sparking meaningful conversations and fostering a community of like-minded creatives.

Mission:At Talkingshirt, our mission is to design and produce high-quality, thought-provoking clothing that inspires self-expression, creativity, and connection. We strive to create a platform where individuals can share their unique voice, challenge the status quo, and celebrate their individuality.
Goal:Our goal is to establish Talkingshirt as a household name, synonymous with bold fashion and unapologetic self-expression. We aim to achieve this by:
* Building a loyal community of 10,000 customers within the first year
* Collaborating with at least 5 influential creatives or brands in the next 6 months
* Expanding our product line to include 20 new designs within the next 12 months
* Achieving an average customer satisfaction rating of 4.5/5 stars within the first 6 months
